{{ page.title }}

October 2018

A collaborative performance developed by Taleen Batalian (Artist) and Heidi Henderson (Dancer/Choreographer) explored connections between the inside and outside of the body and reflected ways vulnerability can be both protective and freeing. Batalian’s sculptural garments informed Henderson’s improvised movement in the exhibition The Phantom of Liberty, RISD Museum, October 2018.
